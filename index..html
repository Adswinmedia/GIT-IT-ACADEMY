About Us

Empowering Careers Since 2012

GIT IT Academy is a premier IT and Computer Training Institute located in the heart of Vidyanagar, Hubli. For over a decade, we have been committed to providing quality education and practical training to students and professionals across Karnataka.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements by offering hands-on training programs designed by industry experts.

Started with a vision to provide quality IT education in Hubli
